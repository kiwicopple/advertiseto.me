Customer Discovery

Advertising is actually a great way to enlarge your audience. When you start a website it's difficult to get traffic, especially if you have no social presence. It's unlikely that any solution will be able to grow an audience as well as current advertising techniques. We hypothesize that it's better to help businesses to grow their audience using these methods and provide them the tools to make sure they are doing it as cheaply and easily as possible.

Most people are apathetic

Outside of the developer community, most consumers simply don't care that they are being tracked. This isn't a problem until the existing platforms decides to use their data nefariously.

Consumers only benefit indirectly.

Advertising is a $400bn industry, with most of the revenue going to just 2 major players. Although the customer indirectly receives benefits, they receive nothing for exposing all their browsing habits, interests, likes, and hobbies.

Solutions

Our plan is to build a new platform where you can specify the type of advertising that you see. Although this has been attempted in the past, we now believe it is possible because of GDPR. We plan to build a platform where you control your data, and only expose non-identifiable information that will allow for targeting.
