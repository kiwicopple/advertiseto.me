Advertise To Me.


We're giving you control over your attention and a chunk of the advertising dollars spent on you. 


Advertising is broken. Somehow, we’ve ended up with 2 major players who trample your privacy, suck up your data, and take home 60% of the digital advertising market. Something’s got to change, and the timing is now. Google and Facebook are facing antitrust probes and GDPR has finally given us access to our data.  We want to build an open-source platform that gives you control over your attention and a chunk of the advertising dollars spent on you. 

What we need to solve

1. There needs to be a simple way for users to file a Subject Access Request which would automatically request data from tech companies.

2. There needs to be a way for users to store their data securely and control how much is exposed to advertisers.

3. There needs to be a way for advertisers to see which audiences are available and easily purchase advertising space.

3. There needs to be a way for users to see how much money they've earned and make transfers to their bank account.

Our next step
